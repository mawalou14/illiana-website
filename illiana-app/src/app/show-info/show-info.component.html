39th Annual Antique Power Exhibition

Important Update

Due to the continuous onset of the coronavirus, the Illiana Board of Directors and members have decided to cancel the 39th Annual Antique Power Show that was scheduled for July 17-19, 2020. Tentatively, the show may be held along with the first Fall Festival/Swap Meet that is planned for September. We hope you all stay safe during this time. As always, this website will post new updates as they become available. Thank you.
