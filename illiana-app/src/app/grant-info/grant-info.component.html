Grant Info

The following projects were made possible through generous grants from North Central Health Services (NCHS) administered by Tippecanoe Arts Federation (TAF).

2019

  • Finalized electrical upgrades throughout the showgrounds
  • Added new concrete porch to the Quilter's building
  • Moved and restored government grain bin to house grain museum
  • Installed waterline from well to outdoor pavilion

2018

  • Purchased 100 banquet chairs
  • Started electrical upgrades throughout the showgrounds
  • Extended fireplace chimney in Assembly Hall
  • Added concrete under railroad tracks at main entrance
  • Replaced 4,000 railroad ties
