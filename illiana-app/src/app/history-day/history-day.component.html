Welcome to History Day!

We here at Illiana are excited to present to you our first ever Virtual History Day! We have created 8 online videos (with more to come) for you to watch at your own pace and learn about key areas of our early ways of life, ranging from the Post Office and Sawmill to the Printing Press and Blacksmithing. Each video was filmed on location at the showgrounds. If you enjoy what you see, come visit us at one of our next events!

To start, simply click the "Begin" button below. You can also view the History Day videos on our YouTube channel.
