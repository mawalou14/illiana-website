We are hosting our second Fall Festival: Swap Meet and Craft Fair! It will be held on the club grounds. Crafters featuring quality homemade items are welcome, along with old iron enthusiasts, engine swappers, and train collectors. The grounds are tree covered with ample parking. Food vendors will be on the grounds. Come join us for a fun-filled day!
