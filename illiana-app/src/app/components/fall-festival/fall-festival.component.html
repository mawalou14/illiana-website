Fall Festival

Saturday, September 23, 2023

9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
View the Flyer

We are hosting our third Fall Festival: Swap Meet and Craft Fair! It will be held on the club grounds. Vendors of arts & crafts, antiques, repurposed furniture, direct sales, swap meet (rusty gold), any type of crafty art are welcome! No flea market items!

This year we have events planned for children's activities, painting classes, and a decorating workshop.

There will be a Bake Sale by Illiana club members, and as always, food vendors!

Two classes will also be available: Wreath-Making and Barn Quilts. The wreath class is $35 with 2 colors to choose from. The barn quilt class is $30 with 3 designs to choose from and a variety of paint colors. See photos below for available designs! For those interested in participaing, please sign up by emailing Kathy Kyle at iapafallfestival@gmail.com and let her know which design you would like to make. Thank you!

Come join us for a fun-filled day!
