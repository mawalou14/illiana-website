Welcome to History Day!

We here at the Illiana Antique Power Association are excited to present to you our first ever Virtual History Day! We have created several online videos for you to watch at your own pace to learn about key areas of our early ways of life, ranging from the Post Office and Sawmill to the Printing Press and Blacksmithing. Each video was filmed on location at the showgrounds by members of our club. If you enjoy what you see, come visit us at one of our next events!
